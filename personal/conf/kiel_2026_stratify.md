Stratified spaces have proven themselves to be a rich and ubiquitous class of mathematical objects, with appearances in diverse areas such as classical algebraic and differential topology and geometry, higher category theory and topological data analysis. With this conference, we aim to foster the exchange of recent advances, ideas and methods between these various communities, working on and with stratified spaces.
