---
title: Rigidity of effective motives in equal characteristic case
author: [Cheni Yuki Yang](/yuki.md)
status: Ongoing
---

We use a motivic tame twist inspired by [Lüd24](/bib/lueders2024tame.md) to construct a spectrum representing $bb(G)_(m)$ in tame motives. As a consequence, we prove a rigidity result for tame motivic homotopy theory after passing to the logarithmic motivic homotopy category. This allows us in particular to construct a synthetic theory of spectra in $p$-adic setting.
