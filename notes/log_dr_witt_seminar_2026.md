The logarithmic de Rham-Witt complex is a useful construction for $p$-adic cohomology, just like the roots of unity for $\ell$-adic cohomology. In this reading seminar, I introduce the original logarithmic de Rham-Witt complex à la [Illusie](/bib/illusie1979dRwitt.md), make it more or less readable and compare it to the complex in [Bhatt-Lurie-Mathew](/bib/blm2021dRwitt.md).
